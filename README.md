Smart devices for women’s health

*Can trends from current fitbit users inform Bellabeat’s marketing strategy?*

**Bellabeat is:**

A high tech company that focuses on women’s health

Determined to empower as many women globally as possible 


**Members are currently:** 

  Tracking calories, activity levels, and movement daily
  Tracking sleep patterns and heart rate
  
**Possible future considerations:**

  Survey users for fitness level 
  
  Survey users to find what they look for in a fitness tracker



**Who**: 
    Data collected from 30 eligible FitBit users 
    
**What**: 
    Minute-level, daily  output for physical activity, heart rate, and sleep monitoring
    
**When**: 
	Data collected from 03/12/2016 - 05/12/2016
    
**How**: 
	Organized by Tracker, Session ID, and  timestamps



**Data integrity and reliability:**

“FitBit Fitness Tracker Data”- Open source at Kaggle.com <sup> 1 </sup>

Cleaned with Excel & BigQuery 

Analyzed with BigQuery

Visualization created in Tableau
